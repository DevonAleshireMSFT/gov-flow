# Governance Templates

Fillable templates for DoD Power Platform governance. Copy and adapt for your organization.

| Template | Purpose | Owner |
|---|---|---|
| [Platform Governance Board Charter](pgb-charter/) | Charter the PGB: scope, composition, authority, cadence | CTO / CIO designee |
| [Environment Register](environment-register/) | Inventory all tenant environments: owner, classification, ATO status, lifecycle dates | Platform CoE |
| [Environment Request Checklist](intake-checklist/) | Intake form fields and validation rules for environment provisioning requests | Platform CoE |
| [ATO Evidence Guide](ato-evidence-guide/) | What evidence to collect, from where, and at what cadence for ATO package preparation | ISSO |

{: .note }
> These templates are DoD-context extensions. For the per-project LP-ALM templates (security role matrix, environment register for a single program), see the [LP-ALM documentation](https://devonaleshiremsft.github.io/layered-platform-alm/).
